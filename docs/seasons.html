Simple Powerball Zone stats.
  • Source data is end-of-game reports scraped from chat logs
  • Only completed games of at least 4v4 size are valid matches
  • Valid matches are split into 500-game seasons
  • Stats are aggregated for any player who participates for at least 80% of the duration of a valid match
  • Any players with fewer than 50 associated valid matches are dropped from the season
  • Gold/Silver/Bronze medals are given for any player with 1st/2nd/3rd-best stats in each category (worth 5/3/2 points)
  • Player aliases are aggregated into their most commonly used nick (for that season)
