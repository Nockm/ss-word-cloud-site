Tier 1: The Antagonists

These players are consistently aggressive, confrontational, or highly critical of others. They frequently engage in insults or disruptive behavior.

    spaceshark

        Temperament: Extremely confrontational and conspiratorial. He frequently accuses others of cheating ("poserware") and rigging the game. He often brings outside politics and aggressive real-life threats (e.g., challenges to a dojo) into the chat.

        Typical Messages:

            "GAME NEEDS A PURGE ON POSERWARE"

            "sign a wavier not to sue after i demolish you in hte dojo"

            "you fiuc n g cheatrs"

    antialiasing (Nin9)

        Temperament: Highly volatile and expressive, often using all caps for emphasis or jest. He frequently clashes with moderators like gunishment and is quick to insult teammates he perceives as "bad" or "ego-centric".

        Typical Messages:

            "PB IS A LOT MORE TOXIC THAN 4V4"

            "gunishments mother is a whore"

            "u guys r idiots"

    zwix

        Temperament: Elitist and easily frustrated by "campers" or "idiots". He relies heavily on his long-term tenure in the game to dismiss others and has a low tolerance for players who do not follow his perceived "principles" of play.

        Typical Messages:

            "as long as i can say 'idiots and morons'.. its ok :)"

            "u read more books than me... u a moron."

            "i in the top tier here. just by the way i play"

    nemesis (Kattster)

        Temperament: Highly critical and prone to lecture-style nagging. While he claims to want a more positive zone, he frequently gets into personal, heated arguments and often labels others as "disruptive trolls" while being equally vocal himself.

        Typical Messages:

            "save the toxicity for league"

            "you're a shitty person who enjoys making people less happy"

            "learn how to treat people, you dumb cunt."

    tool

        Temperament: Blunt, derogatory, and elitist regarding skill and internet quality. He openly admits to team-killing players he dislikes (specifically zwix) as a "strategy" and frequently calls others "retarded".

        Typical Messages:

            "teamkilling zwix is a winning strategy"

            "this guy is fucking retarded"

            "why dont you people pay for better internet"

Tier 2: The Competitive Critics

These players are focused on gameplay and skill, but their frustration often manifests as vocal criticism or sarcasm toward teammates.

    mercury

        Temperament: Sarcastic and skeptical of others' skill or lag. He frequently complains about "hogs" and teammates not playing defense, often taking a "realist" or "cynical" tone toward the state of the game.

        Typical Messages:

            "nah, the best d is prevention"

            "someone ban pez again"

            "u guys suck at cherrypickin lol"

    gunishment

        Temperament: Authoritative and often elitist as a moderator. He is quick to call out "trash" play and frequently tells players to "go back to SVS" if they don't meet his standards, though he occasionally engages in playful banter.

            "need to introduce some kind of elitism to pb"

            "sizzle sells the sausage"

    nabakatsu

            "I know you're a joke because you wont stfu"

            "gotta maintain my chill"

    ucri (Curi)

    the seven year itch

Tier 3: The Casual Players & Banterers

These players generally maintain a neutral or positive attitude, focusing on the social aspect or small talk, though they may occasionally snap in the heat of a game.

    a mirror (Sath)

        Temperament: Generally social and talkative about non-game topics, but can be dismissive of "crying" or complaints from others. He often acts as a moderator for game changes, even if it annoys others.

        Typical Messages:

            "gotta play the safe then sorry approach"

            "god do u cry about everything"

            "i got a blackbelt in slaughtering words"

    no one can kill me

        Temperament: Laid-back, inquisitive, and often uses humor to defuse tension. He frequently asks questions about game mechanics and engages in long, friendly chats about things like pizza or hardware.

        Typical Messages:

            "that's what she said"

            "i like to ignore trolls they just want attention"

            "cognitive bias is real"

    t1c0

            "you guys got a free goal hackers"
